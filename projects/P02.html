Haviland Canvas

This tool was conceived of by the owner of a floral designing business who wanted a way to share ideas with clients before committing to anything concrete.

Project

Floral Design Mock Editor App

Role

Lead Product Designer, UX Researcher

Tools

Sketch, Illustrator, Photoshop

Objective

  • Make it easier for florists to mock up arrangements and ideas.
  • Develop an intuitive tablet first app.

Scope of Work

Research analysis, incorporating feedback from potential users, proposed wireframes, and final design for development.

User Cases

  • Mock-ups for clients
  • Sketches for new ideas
  • Mood board and inspiration creation

Users

Florists

Event Organizers

Hobbyists

DIYers

Interaction Research

Reflecting on popular social media apps, users of tablets are already used to simple photo editing and basic content creation (Instagram, VSCO, Snapchat). Common gestures include swipe, drag and drop, pinch and spread, and 2 point rotate. Sticker and photo editing apps were the closest in functionality to the proposed product.

Modes + Features

  • Create Mode:Choosing arrangement types give designs guides and context. The user can choose flowers based on type, color, name.
  • Database Integration:Flower images are stored as an updatable database that automatically updates to the app.
  • Saved Designs:Drafts and final designs are stored within the app. Images can be exported to be shared.
  • Moodboards:Besided the official flower images, moodboard mode allows the user to upload images to the canvas for inspiration.
